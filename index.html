Why I Built This Project

Typing is a fundamental skill that's more important than ever in today's digital world. We're not just competing with other people anymore – we're competing with AI and automation.

Being able to type quickly and accurately is crucial for productivity, communication, and staying competitive in an increasingly digital workforce. This project is my way of helping people develop this essential skill.

Whether you're a student, professional, or just someone who wants to improve their typing speed, this tool provides a fun and engaging way to practice and measure your progress.

The future belongs to those who can adapt and upskill quickly. By mastering typing, you're not just improving a basic skill – you're building a foundation for success in an AI-driven world.
